About Me!

"To let a sad thought or a bad one get into your mind is as dangerous as letting a scarlet fever germ get into your body." - Frances Hodgeson Burnett, The Secret Garden

A quote from one of my favourite books – which, naturally, is my favourite film adaptation – provides a powerful insight into personal healing, hope and belonging. The story depicts the main characters overcoming their troubles by nurturing their mind by tending to their garden. While I don’t agree with the books’ implication that illnesses are psycho sematic and can be overcome by ‘thinking happy thoughts’ and sheer optimism, the concept of a mind-body connection led me to develop a deep interest in Psychology.
